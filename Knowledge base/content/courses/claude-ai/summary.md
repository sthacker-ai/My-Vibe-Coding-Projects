## 2. Advanced Prompting and Technique

### Iterative Refinement (The Feedback Loop)
Effective interaction with Claude relies on an iterative approach. Instead of expecting a single perfect output, treat the interaction as a feedback loop:
1.  **Establish Context:** Define the role, goal, and constraints clearly at the start.
2.  **Test and Refine:** Ask Claude to generate an initial response, then provide specific, targeted feedback on what needs to be changed (e.g., tone, length, focus).
3.  **Iterate:** Refine the output based on the feedback to achieve the desired complexity.

### Structured Prompting
For complex tasks, use structured prompting techniques to guide the model toward predictable outputs:
*   **Role Assignment:** Assign a specific persona (e.g., "Act as a senior marketing analyst") to set the tone and knowledge base.
*   **Step-by-Step Instructions:** Break down large problems into sequential, manageable steps.
*   **Output Formatting:** Demand specific output structures (e.g., "Respond in JSON format," "Use Markdown tables," or "Provide a five-point bulleted list").
