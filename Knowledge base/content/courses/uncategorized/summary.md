### The Creative Framework

The method for creating a Personal AI Operating System (PAIOS) involves leveraging large language models (LLMs) and software engineering principles to build functional personal agents. This requires a focus on:

### Quantitative Due Diligence Pipeline
The 12-step quantitative methodology enforces a strict pipeline for transforming a trading idea:
1. **Hypothesis:** Define the initial trading idea.
2. **Data Gathering:** Collect and analyze relevant data rigorously.
3. **Analysis:** Apply statistical models and testing to the data.
4. **Validation:** Test the model's robustness and validity against unforeseen risks.
5. **Execution:** Only proceed to execution once all prior gates are passed.
